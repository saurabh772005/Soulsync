🚨 Need Immediate Help?

If you're having thoughts of self-harm, please reach out immediately:

KIRAN: 1800-599-0019 24/7 Mental Health Support
AASRA: 022-27546669 Suicide Prevention Helpline

Immediate Coping Strategies:

  • Take slow, deep breaths
  • Ground yourself: name 5 things you can see, 4 things you can touch
  • Reach out to a trusted friend or family member
  • Remove yourself from stressful situations if possible
  • Remember: this feeling is temporary and will pass
